Welcome to Nick's House of Pizza

Located just steps from Tufts University in Medford, Nick’s House of Pizza has been serving the local community, college students and residents alike, the freshest and tastiest pizza in the area since 1988. With over 35 years of experience in the pizza industry, shop owner Nick Kermanidis keeps his satisfied customers coming back with his hard work, talent and dedication
